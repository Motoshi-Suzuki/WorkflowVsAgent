## address_change

Delivery address changes are permitted only while the order is in **processing** status.
Once an order has been dispatched (state: dispatched, in_transit, or delivered), the
address cannot be changed. The courier already has the parcel and redirection is not
available.

If a customer requests an address change after dispatch, advise them to refuse delivery
so the parcel is returned to sender. A new order or reshipment can then be arranged.

## cancellation

Orders may be cancelled at any point before delivery is confirmed.

- **Before dispatch**: the order is cancelled immediately and a full refund is issued
  within 3–5 business days.
- **After dispatch, before delivery**: the customer must refuse delivery at the door.
  Once the parcel is returned to sender, a full refund is processed within 5–7 business
  days. Do not cancel the courier booking — allow the return-to-sender to complete
  naturally.
- **After delivery**: use the returns process instead.

## refund

Full refunds are issued in the following circumstances:

- Cancelled orders (see cancellation policy).
- Items returned within 30 days of delivery in original, unopened condition.
- Damaged deliveries assessed by the support team (see damage policy).

Refund processing time: 3–5 business days for pre-dispatch cancellations;
5–7 business days for return-to-sender or returns-based refunds.

## returns

Customers may return any item within **30 days** of the delivery date.

Steps:
1. Customer contacts support to initiate the return.
2. A prepaid return label is emailed within 1 business day.
3. Customer ships the item back using the prepaid label.
4. Once received and inspected, a refund or exchange is issued within 5 business days.

Items must be in original packaging. Prepaid labels are single-use and valid for 14 days.

## damage

If a delivery arrives damaged, soaked, or visibly mishandled:

1. The customer is entitled to a **full replacement** or a **full refund** — their choice.
2. This case **must always be routed to a human agent** for goodwill assessment.
   Do not auto-approve eligibility; the support team may offer an additional goodwill
   gesture (discount, priority shipping on the replacement).
3. Ask the customer for their order ID and, if available, a photo of the damage.
4. Estimated resolution: 1–2 business days once damage is confirmed.
